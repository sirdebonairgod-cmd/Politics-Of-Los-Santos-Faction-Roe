Faction Rules of Engagement

Active v1.0 PO-LS Server
📖
What Is a Faction Attack?
5 rules
›
–Faction Attacks occur when a faction goes after another faction causing a hostile environment or putting one or more members from the opposing faction into death mode. The first player to initiate an altercation is part of the attacking faction.
–There must be at least 3 violent interactions (robberies, dissing deceased affiliates, brawls, heavily provoked music, etc.) before you are allowed to perform a lethal attack.
–ALL attacks and defensive attacks must be logged using the attack & defense log format within 24 hours of the attack. A clip must be provided.
–You may not attack or make a lethal interaction with a rival faction 15 minutes before and after server restart.
–Fist fights will not be deemed an attack unless a deadly weapon is pulled (Melee Weapon or Firearm), or unless a faction member stomps on a knocked-out player.

–After 25 interactions with a faction you may KOS — shoot without pinpointing.

–Any attack must wait 8 hours before another lethal attack can be executed. If a faction doesn't retaliate after 48 hours, the opposing faction has rights to attack again.
–After a lethal attack, faction members cannot exit the server until 20 minutes have passed — regardless of whether they are on offense or defense.

Dress Code & Conduct
7 rules
›
1.Faction dress code must be realistic.
2.Hood day clothing is available in server — requires 15 points and must be at least Tier 1 to unlock.
3.Dress accordingly to your faction and role.
4.You do not have to color bang with clothes if you don't want to.
5.Do not walk up to someone and say "what you bang" — talk to them first.
6.If someone is wearing a color, do not walk up to them and ask "do you bang."
7.Learn your faction lingo. If you don't claim your set it will be considered fail RP and a faction strike.

Drill & Combat Rules
14 rules
›
1.Faction attacks involving a weapon cannot be conducted with more than 4 members. No more than 4 weapons on an attack.
2.No block camping.
3.No overhawking — do not hawk beyond your immediate block.
4.No hiding behind fences and bushes.
5.No overly jumping fences.
6.No block wiping. Block wipes are only allowed in Long-Term Wars.
7.No bouncing out front lines only.
8.Do not bounce out 4 deep with guns at PD for no reason.
9.Do not cop bait.
10.No giving green light unless it's your spin — doing so forfeits your protection and opens a faction spin on you.
11.Performing a solo attack on a faction's turf is strictly prohibited.
12.If a faction member shoots out of a vehicle, they MUST shoot in first person — if the clip isn't in first person the shooting is voided.
13.When attacking, you are permitted to kill 2 players max per attack unless the opposing faction fires back — then you may attempt to kill defending shooters.
14.Masks should only be worn when sliding. If a PD officer asks you to remove your mask, you must do so.

Death & Cooldowns
5 rules
›
–The cooldown for any lethal attack on enemies is 8 hours unless a mutual agreement is made by both faction leaders during a Long-Term War.
–Players who CK/PK during a faction attack are automatically considered dead and have no recollection of the attack or events leading up to it.
–It takes 1 headshot to CK a player. To CK someone killed by body shots, you must stand over them and shoot them in the head while they are on the floor.
–After sliding on an opposing faction, your faction must go play defense on your block for at least 45 minutes or more.
–There is a 3 day wait before joining any faction after being CK'd. Use this time for character development. Every kill must be clipped — killer POV is mandatory.
⚔️
Long-Term War Guidelines
10 rules
›
Wars must be approved OOC by Faction Management. Both factions must have leadership sign a war agreement.
1.Must be approved OOC by Faction Management — sufficient evidence of conflict must be shown.
2.The war must be mutual between the two factions — one faction may not apply alone.
3.Wars must have sufficient roleplay built up behind them. Factions must reach Tier 3 first.
4.Faction attacks during LTW must wait 7 hours from the end of the previous attack before the next can occur.
5.Block Wipes are permitted in LTW but must be roleplayed correctly — you cannot kill every member on that faction's roster right away.
6.Attack and defense logs must still be filed accordingly even during a LTW.
7.Character Killed members can rejoin the war on a new character.
8.Allied factions cannot be brought into a Long-Term War.
9.The Faction Management team has the right to end any LTW if it gets out of hand.
10.A war can only end if both faction leaders agree to end it. Any custom rules must be brought to Faction Management for approval.

Armor, Reporting & Misc Rules
8 rules
›
1.The use of body armor is restricted to a maximum of 15% armor during faction-related activities.
2.You are not allowed to report another faction for an incident that occurred more than 3 days ago.
3.Reporting for revenge because a situation didn't go as planned is forbidden.
4.If you are not a member of a faction, you are not able to participate in a faction attack.
5.You must wait at least 72 hours (3 days) after joining a faction or creating a new character before you can perform an attack.
6.When reporting an opposing faction you must cease all lethal roleplay with each other. No reason to be sliding on someone you are reporting.
7.You cannot play as a female character if you are not a real life female.
8.Manhunting of any sort is punishable — do not chase anyone throughout their hood in an attempt to murder them.

Probationary Period
2 rules
›
–Factions on probation are not able to initiate attacks during those 3 days. If another faction attacks you, you have the right to defend.
–You may not attack a faction that is on probation unless they bring it upon themselves (disrespecting the dead, disrespecting your gang).
